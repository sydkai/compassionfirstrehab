FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I get Started

  • Call us at 908-499-0208 or fill out the contact form. We will reach out and verify your insurance coverage.
  • For Medicare B patients, we can assist in getting a prescription/ script from your primary care physician.

When can I start Physical Therapy?

  • If Medicare B is your primary insurance, our services can be initiated at any time upon endorsement of a referral/ prescription from your healthcare provider.
  • If your primary insurance is a commercial insurance, we don't need a prescription to start your initial evaluation.
  • We can start your out-patient therapy after an injury, discharge from the hospital, nursing home, or home health care
  • We can also treat clients with general mobility or pain issues without a medical event or injury.

WHERE CAN I BE SEEN? DO I NEED TO BE HOME-BOUND?

  • You can be seen in the comfort and privacy of your home, office, gym, adult day program, or senior community.
  • Our clients may or may not be home-bound, with frequency and duration tailored to their needs and goals.

WILL INSURANCE COVER THE SERVICES?

  • Yes. Our services are covered by Medicare Part B, Medicare Advantage PPOs, and commercial/ private insurances.
  • We will verify your insurance coverage since every plan varies.

    • DO YOU OFFER SELF-PAY SERVICES?

    • Yes. We have affordable packages that will suit your rehab needs. For more questions on self-pay rates, please call us at (908) 499-0208.

    WHERE IS YOUR AREA OF COVERAGE?​

    • We proudly serve the counties of Somerset, Morris, Middlesex counties and neighboring communities. As rehab referrals grow, we can cover more areas soon.

    WHAT IS YOUR CANCELLATION OR APPOINTMENT CHANGE POLICY?

    • Please call your therapist by 5:00 p.m. on the day before your scheduled appointment. To cancel a Monday appointment, please call by 5:00 p.m. on the Saturday prior.
    • Late cancellations and no-shows are subject to $50.00 fee.
