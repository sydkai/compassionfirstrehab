    CORVIRUS CONCERNS?

    YOUR SAFETY IS OUR TOP PRIORITY

    During this challenging time, CompassionFirst Rehab LLC was conceptualized and eventually formed. We remain committed in serving patients in need of therapy. By delivering physical therapy services to your homes, we reduce your exposure to the novel coronavirus.

    We are accepting new patients and continue to see existing patients in their homes, offices, gyms, and senior communities. We take the safety of our patients, their respective families, caregivers and our staff very seriously. Our therapists are tested every two weeks. We screen our patients accordingly and take the necessary precautions to ensure safety. We follow CDC Guidelines for Health Care Providers and regularly provide updates in the event our therapists display COVID-19 symptoms or become exposed to confirmed COVID-19 positive individuals.

    If you think you have been exposed to COVID‑19 and develop fever and symptoms such as cough or difficulty breathing, call your healthcare provider the soonest.

    About CompassionFirst Rehab

    CompassionFirst Rehab LLC is a therapist-owned company which provides in-home and virtual outpatient therapy services. We pride ourselves in providing the highest quality of care to enable you to return to your normal activities the soonest, prevent further injuries, improve overall quality of life and help you stay active. Our team of dedicated professionals has been treating patients with integrity, devotion and compassion. Reach out today to learn more about our full range of services
